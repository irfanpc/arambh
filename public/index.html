About Our School

20+ Years of
Excellence

Nurturing Young Minds

At Aarambh School, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a supportive environment to explore, learn, and grow. Our playful approach fosters creativity and confidence.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality early education in a warm, caring, and secure setting.

Our Vision

To inspire a lifelong love for learning and create responsible future citizens.

Our Philosophy

Learning through play, compassion, and hands-on experiences.

Our Programs

Tailored learning stages designed for holistic development.

Baby Nursery

  • 2 to 3 years — Playgroup
  • 3 to 4 years — Nursery

Focuses on sensory development, motor skills, and foundational social interaction.

LKG

  • 4 to 5 years — LKG
  • 5 to 6 years — UKG

Introduces basic alphabets, numbers, and creative arts in an engaging environment.

UKG

  • 6 to 7 years — Grade 1
  • 7 to 8 years — Grade 2

Prepares children with foundational reading, writing, and logical skills.

Primary Classes

  • 8 to 9 years — Grade 3
  • 9 to 10 years — Grade 4
  • 10 to 11 years — Grade 5

Prepares your child to soar into higher classes with ease and confidence.

Admission Process

Joining the Aarambh family is simple and straightforward. We welcome parents to visit our campus and experience our joyful environment firsthand.

  • 1
    School Tour Schedule a visit and meet our lovely educators.
  • 2
    Application Form Fill out the form online or at our reception desk.
  • 3
    Interaction Session A brief, friendly meeting with the child and parents.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate of the child
  • 2 Passport size photographs
  • Aadhaar Card copy of the child and parents
